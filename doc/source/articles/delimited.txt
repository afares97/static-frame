



Extending Python's CSV-Reader C-Extension to Make A High-Performance, Delimited-Text-to-Numpy-Array Converter

A (Mostly) Faster-than-Pandas CSV Reader with Support for All NumPy Array Types








CSV and related delimted text file formats remain a necessary evil. While such formats are comonly used to represent tabular data, they lack type definitions. While numerous high-performance binary alternatives exist (such as Parquet or NPZ), CSV is still popular: it is human readabile and can be loaded in Excel and many other libraries.
